# Product

## Register
product

## Users
Professional accessibility remediators and technical/legal document reviewers who spend 8-10 hours a day inside RAWRS verifying and repairing PDF→DOCX accessibility compliance (WCAG). Expert, repeat, high-frequency users — not occasional visitors.

## Product Purpose
RAWRS is a professional Accessibility Verification & Document Remediation Workstation — not a PDF editor, DOCX editor, Markdown editor, or OCR tool. It exists so remediators can detect, review, compare, repair, verify, and export accessibility fixes for one document at a time, with the document itself always dominating the interface. Success = a reviewer never thinks "I am navigating pages," only "I am reviewing accessibility."

## Brand Personality
Systematic, authoritative, calm-under-density. The combined instincts of Apple, Google Material, Adobe Acrobat, Linear, Cursor, JetBrains, GitHub, and Figma — precision tooling for experts, not a consumer app.

## Anti-references
Bootstrap-style admin dashboards, Jira, SharePoint, generic analytics dashboards, enterprise CRUD shells, hero-metric SaaS dashboard cards, multi-tenant SaaS marketing funnels (sign-in/pricing/trials) — RAWRS is local-first and single-reviewer by deliberate architecture decision, not a gap.

## Design Principles
- The document dominates; chrome exists only to support reviewing it.
- One interaction model per workflow — extend proven patterns before inventing new ones.
- Every click, second, and cognitive decision removed is a real win at 8-10hr/day scale.
- Never look AI-generated: intentional hierarchy over generic cards/metrics/gradients.
- Accessibility is not a feature of this product, it is the product — its own UI must model the standard it enforces.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG 2.2 AA minimum for RAWRS's own interface (the product audits others against this standard and must not fail it itself). Full keyboard operability, screen-reader-correct semantics, reduced-motion support, no color-only signaling.
